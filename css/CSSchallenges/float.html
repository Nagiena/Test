Gretel

Gretel is one of the main protagonists in Secret Magic Control Agency, a Netflix animated film. She is a skilled agent working for the Secret Magic Control Agency, responsible for solving magical crimes. Intelligent, brave, and resourceful, Gretel is assigned to retrieve the kidnapped King alongside her estranged brother, Hansel. Unlike Hansel, who is a charming con artist, Gretel is disciplined and dedicated to her duty. Throughout their adventure, she uses her expertise in magic and detective work to uncover the mystery while rekindling her bond with Hansel.
